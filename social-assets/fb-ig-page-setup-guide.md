## Part 3: Connect Pages to Meta Ads Manager

Once both pages are created:

1. Go to **business.facebook.com**
2. Navigate to **Business Settings → Accounts → Pages**
3. Click **Add → Add a Page** → select **Deep Sleep Reset**
4. Navigate to **Business Settings → Accounts → Instagram Accounts**
5. Click **Add → Connect Your Instagram Account**
6. Log in to your Instagram account

This connects both pages to your ad account so you can run ads from both placements.
