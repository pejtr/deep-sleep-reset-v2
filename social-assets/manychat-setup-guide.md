## What You'll Build

When someone comments **"SLEEP"** (or any keyword) on your Instagram post, ManyChat automatically sends them a DM with your checkout link. This is one of the highest-converting Instagram tactics for digital products — comment-to-DM flows typically convert at 15–30%.

---

## Step 1 — Create a Free ManyChat Account

1. Go to **[manychat.com](https://manychat.com)**
2. Click **"Get Started Free"**
3. Sign up with your Facebook account (the one connected to your Instagram Business page)
4. Select **Instagram** as your channel

---

## Step 2 — Connect Your Instagram Account

1. In ManyChat dashboard → **Settings** → **Instagram**
2. Click **"Connect Instagram Account"**
3. Select **@deep.sleep.reset**
4. Grant all requested permissions (comments, messages, etc.)

> **Important:** Your Instagram must be a **Business Account** connected to a Facebook Page. If it's still a Personal account, go to Instagram Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account → Business.

---

## Step 3 — Create the "Comment to DM" Flow

### 3a. Create a New Flow

1. Go to **Flows** → **New Flow**
2. Name it: `Comment Trigger — SLEEP`
3. Click **"+ Add Trigger"**

### 3b. Set the Trigger

1. Select **"Instagram Comment"**
2. Choose: **"Specific post"** or **"Any post"** (start with Any post)
3. Set keyword: **`SLEEP`** (or multiple: SLEEP, INFO, LINK, YES)
4. Match type: **"Contains"**

### 3c. Build the DM Message

Click **"+ Add Message"** → **"Send DM"**

Paste this message:

```
Hey {{first name}}! 👋

Thanks for your comment! Here's the link to the 7-Night Deep Sleep Reset — just $5 and you can start tonight:

👉 https://deep-sleep-reset.com

It's backed by CBT-I science (the same method sleep doctors use) and comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee.

If you have any questions, just reply here! 🌙
```

### 3d. Add a Public Comment Reply (Optional but recommended)

After the DM step, add **"Reply to Comment"**:

```
Sent you a DM with the link! 💌
```

This makes the automation visible to other viewers, which increases social proof and encourages more people to comment.

---

## Step 4 — Activate and Test

1. Click **"Publish"** in the top right
2. Go to your Instagram post → comment **"SLEEP"** from a test account
3. Check that you receive the DM within 30 seconds
