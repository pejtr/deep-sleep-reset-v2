## Phase 1: Account Creation (Day 1)

### Step 1 — Create a fresh Instagram account

Use a device or browser that has **never been logged into any personal Instagram account**. This prevents Meta from linking Luna's account to your personal profile and showing her content to people who know you.

1. Open Instagram on a new device, or use a browser in private/incognito mode
2. Sign up with a **new email address** dedicated to Luna (e.g., `luna.sleep.reset@gmail.com`)
3. Username: `luna.sleep.reset` (or first available from the priority list)
4. Do **not** connect to Facebook, sync contacts, or link any personal accounts during setup

### Step 2 — Convert to Professional Account

1. Go to Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account
2. Select **Creator** (not Business — Creator gets better organic reach)
3. Category: **Health/Beauty** or **Education**
4. Do **not** add a phone number from your personal SIM

### Step 3 — Profile Setup

**Profile photo:** Upload `luna-profile-main.png`

**Name field:** `Luna | Sleep Coach 🌙`

**Bio (copy exactly):**
```
Helping you sleep like you used to 🌙
CBT-I based • Science-backed • Actually works
🤖 AI-powered coach
↓ Free 7-night sleep reset
```

**Link in bio:** `https://deep-sleep-reset.com`

**Note on AI disclosure:** Meta's 2024 policy requires AI-generated personas to be clearly labeled. The "🤖 AI-powered coach" line in the bio fulfills this requirement and actually builds trust with a tech-savvy audience.

## Phase 2: Profile Warmup (Days 1–14)

The warmup period is critical. A new Instagram account has a "trust score" that determines how widely content is distributed. Rushing this phase causes Shadow Ban — where posts get 0–3 views regardless of quality.

### Daily Warmup Routine (10 minutes/day)

| Time | Action |
|---|---|
| Morning | Open app, scroll feed for 3–5 minutes, like 5–10 posts in the sleep/wellness niche |
| Afternoon | Watch 3–5 Reels fully (don't skip) — this signals you're a real user |
| Evening | Post content (see schedule below), then stay active for 10 minutes after posting |

### What NOT to do in the first 14 days
- Do not add the product link to bio until Day 7 (Instagram flags new accounts with affiliate/product links)
- Do not use scheduling tools — post manually
- Do not follow more than 20 accounts per day
- Do not use any automation tools for likes, follows, or comments
- Do not post more than 2 posts per day

### Warmup Content Schedule (Days 1–14)

**Days 1–3:** Post only Stories (2/day). No Reels yet. Stories: introduce Luna, share a sleep tip, ask a poll question.

**Days 4–7:** Add 1 Reel every 2 days. Keep it short (15–30 seconds). Use trending audio.

**Days 8–14:** Post 1 Reel/day + 2 Stories/day. Start adding the product link to bio on Day 7.

## Phase 5: DM Automation Setup

### Option A — Manual (Free, immediate)
Respond to every DM personally using the scripts in `LUNA_PERSONA.md`. Takes 15–30 min/day. Best for first 30 days to understand what questions people actually ask.

### Option B — ManyChat (Recommended, ~$15/month)
ManyChat connects to Instagram via official Meta API and automates keyword-triggered DM responses.

**Setup:**
1. Go to manychat.com → Connect Instagram account
2. Create keyword triggers: "3AM", "help", "can't sleep", "price", "how much", "info", "link"
3. For each trigger, set the response from `LUNA_PERSONA.md` DM Scripts section
4. Enable "Default Reply" for unrecognized messages (generic fallback script)
5. Set delay: 30–90 seconds (instant replies look robotic)

### Option C — Built-in Admin Dashboard Bot (Coming in Phase 4)
The Deep Sleep Reset admin dashboard will include a Luna Bot tab with full conversation management, keyword triggers, and AI-generated responses using the Gemini API.

## Phase 6: Comment Strategy

### The "DM Funnel" Comment Method
On every Reel, include a CTA that drives people to DM. When someone comments asking for the link or more info, reply publicly to move them to DM:

> "Sending you a DM now! 🌙"

This creates social proof (others see you're responsive) and moves the conversation to a private channel where conversion is easier.

### Pinned Comment Strategy
On your top-performing posts, pin a comment like:
> "For everyone asking — yes, the full 7-night protocol is at the link in my bio. It's $5 and has a 30-day guarantee 🌙💙"
