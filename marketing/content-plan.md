# Deep Sleep Reset — Marketing Content Plan

## 3 FB/IG Reels — Content Plan (A/B Testing Hooks)

---

### Reel #1 — "The Brain Recovery Hook" (Loss Aversion)
**Target:** Cold traffic, Tier 2 (India, Philippines, Nigeria) + Tier 1 (US, UK)
**Duration:** 25–30 seconds
**Format:** Text-on-screen + voiceover + ambient sleep visuals

**Hook (0–3s — make or break):**
> "Every night you sleep wrong, you lose 40% of your brain's recovery. Here's how to fix it in 7 nights."

**Script:**
- 0–3s: Hook text on black screen, bold white font. Voiceover reads it.
- 3–8s: "Scientists call it chronotype — your biological sleep window. Most people sleep outside it."
- 8–15s: "When you sleep at the wrong time for YOUR body, you wake up exhausted even after 8 hours."
- 15–22s: "I took a free 60-second quiz and discovered my chronotype. Night 1 I slept deeper than I had in years."
- 22–28s: CTA — "Take the free quiz — link in bio. Takes 60 seconds."

**Visual style:** Dark background, purple/indigo accent text, slow-motion sleep footage (royalty-free), star field or brain scan overlay.

**Caption:**
> Why do you wake up tired after 8 hours? Your chronotype is the answer. Free quiz in bio — takes 60 seconds. #sleep #chronotype #deepsleep #sleephack #brainhealth

**Expected performance:** Highest stop-rate due to loss aversion framing ("you LOSE 40%"). Best for cold audiences.

---

### Reel #2 — "The Identity Hook" (Curiosity + Self-Discovery)
**Target:** Tier 1 (US, UK, AU, CA) — higher disposable income, more likely to buy
**Duration:** 20–25 seconds
**Format:** Quiz-style interactive feel, fast cuts

**Hook (0–3s):**
> "Are you a Lion, Bear, Wolf, or Dolphin? Your sleep type determines everything."

**Script:**
- 0–3s: Hook — 4 animal icons appear one by one.
- 3–8s: "Your chronotype is your biological sleep personality. It controls when you should sleep, wake, and work."
- 8–14s: Show each type briefly: "Lions wake at 5am naturally. Bears follow the sun. Wolves come alive at midnight. Dolphins never fully sleep deeply."
- 14–20s: "Which one are you? Free quiz reveals your type and gives you a 7-night reset plan — link in bio."

**Visual style:** Animated animal icons, dark background, fast-paced cuts, upbeat but calm music.

**Caption:**
> Lion? Bear? Wolf? Dolphin? Your chronotype is why you sleep the way you do. Free quiz in bio — find out yours in 60 seconds. #chronotype #sleeptype #sleepscience #wellness

**Expected performance:** Highest share rate — people tag friends. Best for organic reach amplification.

---

### Reel #3 — "The Social Proof Hook" (Authority + Results)
**Target:** Retargeting warm audiences who saw Reel #1 or #2
**Duration:** 30–35 seconds
**Format:** Testimonial-style, text overlays on results

**Hook (0–3s):**
> "I fixed my sleep in 7 nights for $1. Here's exactly what I did."

**Script:**
- 0–3s: Hook on screen. Voiceover: "I spent years waking up exhausted. Tried everything."
- 3–10s: "Then I took a free chronotype quiz. Turns out I was sleeping 3 hours outside my biological window."
- 10–18s: "I followed the 7-Night Deep Sleep Reset — a simple $1 guide based on my chronotype. By night 3, I was sleeping through the night."
- 18–25s: "12,847 people have taken the quiz. Average rating: 4.9 stars."
- 25–32s: CTA — "Link in bio. Free quiz, then the guide is just $1. Less than a coffee."

**Visual style:** Clean, minimal, white text on dark. Progress bar showing "Night 1 → Night 7". Real-looking transformation.

**Caption:**
> Fixed my sleep in 7 nights for $1. The chronotype quiz changed everything. Link in bio — free quiz, $1 guide. #sleep #sleepfix #chronotype #wellness #healthtips

**Expected performance:** Highest conversion rate — direct price mention removes mystery, $1 is irresistible.
